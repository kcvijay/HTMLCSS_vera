About me

Few Words

LSince arriving in Finland from Nepal in 2012, I have already completed BBA, almost graduating an MBA and have experiences of doing all kinds of jobs such as, cleaner, gardener, postman, bar-worker, kebab-factory worker, gasket-factory worker, all kinds of minial jobs. I might have lived a satisfactory life in past but, never got a true satisfaction, from a career perspective. I realized late but, not so late that I need a career that I can spend my precious time on and not regret in deathbed. I need a work where Monday mornings will feel like going on an adventure not like going on a battlefield, with a hangover. Thus I decided in December 2021, to stop whatever I am doing, leave from a comfort zone, disregard the debt-trap and focus on a career. Website making is always my goto place after a hectic and tiring work day. It is like a therapy. If I am bored or needed a distraction, I log on VS Code and start typing. Making websites has kept me sane, in a way. One day I questioned myself, If I enjoy website producing this much, why not making this a profession? That self-realization has led me here. I have spend months self-teaching myself fundamentals of web desiging and have enrolled to Fullstack Web Developer Program in React and Node, which will more broaden and solidify the current expertise.
